This 31 year old, somewhat avant garde style, Northbay resident has been creating eye catching graphics for 13 years and recently branched out into front-end web design. Her most common form and potter. Talitha is deeply passionate about being forward moving, genuine to self, and uniquely creative.

She creates amazing visual displays which people tend to stare at length, as her abstracts are visual escapes and playgrounds for imagination to run wildly through. "T" is planning her debut artist opening show titled: Escape the Ordinary, which is filled with a colorful world of abstractions. Show details TBA.

Ideally, Talitha prefers to collaborate with like minded people who have meaningful projects that she can contribute to and improve the quality of. She's currently devoted to bettering her knowledge in front end web development with an emphasis on responsive design, custom illustrations, general print graphics, and photo retouching.
