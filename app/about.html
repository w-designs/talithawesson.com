This avant-garde, California native, Northbay area resident, has been creating eye catching graphics for 13 years. Her most common form of creative expression is abstract art, which she is striving to mix into the world of realism. In earlier years she was a photographer and an electric wheel potter. The world of creativity is amongst her highest joys. Talitha is also deeply passionate about being forward moving and genuine to self.

She creates amazing visual displays which people tend to stare at for great lengths of time, letting their imagination sink into her visual escapes and mental playgrounds. Let your imagination run wildly through her art to see what you can find. "T" is planning her debut artist opening show titled: Escape the Ordinary, which is filled with a colorful world of abstractions. Show details TBA.

Ideally, Talitha prefers to collaborate casually, with down to earth people, on meaningful projects that she can contribute to and improve the quality of. She's currently devoted to bettering her knowledge of front end web development with an emphasis on responsive design, custom illustrations, as well as general print graphics, and photo retouching.
