This 31 year old, Northbay resident has been creating graphics for 14 years and recently branched out into front-end web design. She specializes in abstract art, but in her earlier days she was a photographer and potter. Talitha is deeply passionate about being forward moving, genuine to self, and uniquely creative.

She creates visual displays which people tend to stare at length, as her abstracts are visual escapes and playgrounds for imagination to run wildly through. "T" is planning her debut artist opening show titled: Escape the Ordinary, which is filled with a colorful world of abstractions. Show details TBA.

Ideally, Talitha prefers to collaborate with like minded people who have meaningful projects that she can contribute to and improve the quality of. She contributes herself to front end web development with an emphasis on Responsive Design. Custom illustrations from her abstract imagination, general print graphics, and photo retouching.

“Life is a dance & I create my own steps.”
