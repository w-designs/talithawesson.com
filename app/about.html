About Talitha

Talitha Wesson is a highly creative, quality oriented artist and graphic designer. Over the last ten+ years her goal has been consistent: visual success. “I always design with the visual sweet spot as my goal; a design is not done until it pops.”

Native to California, she has cultivated much creativity from her time spent amongst Redwoods Forests and Norcal beaches. Now, 2015 brings her into a new journey in San Francisco and Berkeley's art culuture.

“I have fallen in love with mixed media illustrations and identity design.” As she paves the way for her new style, she also stays true to her graphic design layout roots.

She also dabbles in static web design mostly for the purpose of bridging the gap between programers and designers. -These two types must work together, but often speak very different languages. Talitha has devoted much effort to tech discussions and understanding the main differences between the work of programers and creatives.
