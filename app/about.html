Talitha Wesson brings a fresh perspective into each new project, as creativity is amongst her highest joys. She has been a quality oriented graphic designer for over 10 years. With a passion for producing eye catching displays for her clients, she strives to make every project a visual success. Whether her chosen mediums are watercolors or the pen tool in illustrator, her efforts are sure to please the eye. She is self propelled to learn new things that improve the efficiency of her workflow.

Her style spans across abstract and avant-garde styles to the more widely accepted norms of modern design, including both 3D and flat design. During her time as a pre-press operator she learned the importance of creating quality files, and understands how to make her designs work at each phase throughout the process of printing. She creates extremely high quality work with pixel precision and goes the extra mile to make sure each project is done well.

Talitha thrives under deadlines and has the ability to manage the timing of multiple projects at once. Recently new skills have brought her into deeper levels of design by way of learning advanced tools, new techniques and design functionality. Since getting into higher levels of illustration, graphic and web design, she brings more to the table than ever before.
