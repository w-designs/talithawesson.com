Talitha Wesson is highly creative and quality oriented. With over ten years of graphic design experience, her goal is to make each project snap. "Every piece of art has a visual sweet spot, it's not done until that spot is found."

This California native has spent time living amongst the Redwoods in the North Bay area and has now embarked on a new journey in San Francisco. In addition to graphic design, her artistic focus has gone from abstract to whimsical realism.

"I have fallen in love with mixed media illustrations and branding." As she paves the way for her new style, she stays true to graphic layout roots. She also dabbles in static web design.
