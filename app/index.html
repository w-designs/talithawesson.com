3d Illustrations

There is definitely a place for flat design and I love how the new trends are looking, but I also want to explore the world of realism like I never have before. I have never been interested in the ordinary aspects of the world, which is why my art is normally heavily abstract. It's a far more interesting world, that of things made up. There is more to awe about, dream of, and be inspired by. For the next few months, my plan is to develop my style of realism and then merge the two styles. It's inspiring to branch out into other styles of art. 3D for you, 3D from me!


"I cannot seem to break the gossamer veil that lies between realism and abstraction and I am forced to search for meaning between the two states of consciousness." -John Cerlienco, Artist


Passionate Emphasis

I finally feel like I've stuck with Graphic Design long enough to gain the skills needed for my passions to really take off. Simply having creativity is not enough alone. A creative individual needs the right skills and platform to express creativity successfully. My passion is soaring up with pure joy and excitement for where I'm headed next. I've chosen to delve more deeply into the world of high quality SVG (effects via code) and Parallax Web Design (that works). Not all Parallax is worth it, but I feel like I can make it work. I have plans of how to create the most fantastic aspects of Parallax Design without enormous bandwidth and custom altered responsive mobile versions.
