New Realistic Illustrations

There is definitely a place for flat design, and even though I love its latest trends I'm also exploring the world of realism at a deeper level. I had not previously been interested in ordinary aspects of the world, which is why my art has been heavily abstract. I had found it far more interesting, there in that world of imagination... A place where everything is something to awe about, dream of, and be inspired by. However, for the next few months I'll be focused on further developing my realism skills. Subsequently, those designs will then be merged into my normal whimsical abstractions. It's inspiring to branch out into other styles of art, it makes tomorrow ever enticing. 3D for you, 3D from me!

"I cannot seem to break the gossamer veil that lies between realism and abstraction and I am forced to search for meaning between the two states of consciousness." -John Cerlienco, Artist

Web Update: Passionate Fork in the Road

I am proud to have stuck with Graphic Design long enough to gain the skills needed for my passions to really take off. I am finally at the level I've always dreamed of. Of course there are hurdles and mountains yet to climb, but I've traveled many distances. That sweet spot where designing feels like pedaling a bike down a smooth street with the wind blowing in your face. Simply having creativity was never enough to make me feel that good (sunshine on your face kind of good). That wonky creativity had to be squeezed through some lengthy disciplines in order for the value to begin to shine through the random creative chaos. Creative individuals, such as myself, need the right skills and platform to be able to express their creativity successfully. My passion is soaring up with excitement for so many new ventures. One, as of late: I've chosen to delve more deeply into the world of high quality SVG (effects via code) and Parallax Web Design (that actually works). From what I hear, not all Parallax is worth the effort. I have the drive to make it work. Current plans are unfolding for how to create the best aspects of Parallax Design without the enormous bandwidth. Custom altered responsive mobile versions should solve it, but we shall wait and see...
